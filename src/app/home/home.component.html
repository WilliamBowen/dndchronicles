This website is currently being constructed to act as a collection of journals for characters that are playing in the Dungeons and Dragons campaign that I am running. My players will be given access to the site and will be able to tell the stories of their adventures through the eyes of their characters.

For now the only options I have implemented are user registration and login. Eventually I will be adding features that will allow me to create a campaign, add sessions to that campaign, and then allow my players to create characters for the campaign, and log their stories for each session.

Beyond that, I would like to expand things so that other Dungeon Masters can use this site with their own players. I believe this site will allow them to gain a better understanding of their players, and will let the DMs better run their campaigns.
